❓ 9. FAQ – Energy Supplement Questions People Are Asking

Can I take Moringa and coffee together?

Yes. Moringa is caffeine-free and may help buffer caffeine crashes when taken alongside coffee.

Are NAD+ boosters safe to take every day?

Yes — when used as directed and purchased from reputable sources. Look for GMP or NSF certifications.

When will I start feeling the effects?

Most users report noticeable energy and focus within 5 to 10 days. Adaptogens may take up to 2 weeks.

Can I stack supplements like Citicoline and NALT?

Yes, especially for cognitive support. Always start with low doses and consult your provider if unsure.

Who should avoid Rhodiola?

Anyone with bipolar disorder or on mood medications should consult a doctor before using Rhodiola.

What side effects can energy supplements cause?

Some supplements—especially those with caffeine or synephrine—can cause jitteriness, heart palpitations, anxiety, or digestive upset. Opt for products with third‑party testing to minimize risk.

How do I know if a supplement is high quality and safe?

Supplements aren’t FDA-approved before sale. Look for GMP certification, NSF or USP third-party testing, and full Supplement Facts labeling.

Can energy supplements interact with my medications?

Yes—especially stimulant or adaptogen-based formulas, which may affect heart rate, blood pressure, or mood medications. Always check with your doctor or pharmacist.

Will supplements work better along with lifestyle changes?

Absolutely. A balanced diet, proper sleep, hydration, and exercise create the foundation—supplements can support, not replace, these basics.

Can I take energy supplements during pregnancy or breastfeeding?

Most energy formulas haven’t been extensively tested during pregnancy or nursing. It's best to consult your healthcare provider before using them in these life stages.
